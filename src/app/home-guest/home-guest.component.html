Hello dear Guest, welcome to our little project!

The application has been built to ease the management of people living together.

If you are a part of a family, living with a roommate, or just want to keep track of the bills you are paying,this app would be perfect for you!

Currently, we support the following features:

  • Summary Dashboard with all the people and their current bills.

  • List of all the bills, sorted by months.

  • Personal section, for your bills that havent been payed yet.
